Power BI Infographic Report: Global Displacement and Climate Impact 2023

This project leverages data from the Internal Displacement Monitoring Centre (IDMC) to analyze global displacement trends caused by natural disasters from 2008 to 2023. Through interactive Power BI dashboards, it showcases key insights, including the displacement of 13 million people in 2023, flood-induced displacements averaging 127,000 per event, and significant regional disparities like Sudan's rate being three times the global average. The analysis aims to support climate justice initiatives and enhance understanding of the human impact of environmental crises.

Key Insights:

  • Global Displacement Overview:13 million people displaced in 2023, with floods averaging 127,000 displaced per event.
  • Regional Disparities:Sudan's displacement rate was 3x the global average, highlighting disproportionate impacts on vulnerable regions.
  • Trends and Patterns:Year-over-year analysis revealed significant increases in displacement tied to climate-driven events.

Project Impact:

  • Informed Policy Development: Delivered actionable insights to support climate justice and resource allocation..
  • Stakeholder Engagement: Simplified complex data for non-technical audiences using interactive visualizations..
  • Enhanced Awareness: Highlighted critical areas of intervention in addressing global displacement challenges..
