Power BI dashboard: Analyzing Africa's Tech Funding Landscape

This Power BI dashboard provides an in-depth analysis of Africa's tech funding landscape from 2010 to 2023. It highlights key metrics, including $13.6B in total funding, $7.14B in latest sector funding, and the dominance of Life Sciences with $2.1B in recent investments. Explore sectoral trends, funding distribution, and insights into investor impact across the continent..

Key Insights:

  • Life Sciences Lead:Dominated funding with $2.1B in latest funding, contributing significantly to the $13.6B total funding across sectors.
  • Sectoral Funding Trends: Latest reported funding across all sectors reached $7.14B, reflecting substantial investor interest in emerging industries.
  • Revenue Analysis: Six companies disclosed a combined annual revenue of $348M, showcasing an average of $101.41M per angel investor's contribution.

Project Impact:

  • Informed Investment Decisions: Enabled stakeholders to identify high-performing sectors and allocate resources effectively..
  • Enhanced Data Accessibility: Delivered a user-friendly dashboard that distilled complex data into actionable insights for both technical and non-technical audiences.
  • Strategic Planning Support: Provided a comprehensive view of funding patterns to guide strategic business and policy planning..
