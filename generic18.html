Looker Studio dashboard: Visualizing revenue, sales , profit etc for Fast food items sold

This Looker Studio dashboard involved analyzing sales, revenue, costs, and profitability data for a fast food chain across multiple locations, with a focus on uncovering trends and actionable insights to optimize operational efficiency and decision-making.

Key Insights:

  • Purchase Type:Highlighted that walk-in purchases were the most profitable purchase type, contributing $327K of total revenue.
  • Manager Performance: Discovered that cities managed by Manager Joao Silva consistently outperformed others, achieving a 30% higher profit margin.
  • Payment Method:Found that credit card payments were the highest, accounting for $400K of total transactions, emphasizing the need to streamline card payment processes.

Project Impact:

  • This analysis provided actionable insights for management to improve profitability, streamline operations, and enhance customer satisfaction. By leveraging data-driven strategies, the fast food chain could make informed decisions to optimize resource allocation, adjust pricing strategies, and enhance overall performance across all locations.
